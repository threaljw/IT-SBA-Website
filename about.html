Our Mission

Paul's Health Awareness is dedicated to educating people about major illnesses, their symptoms, and treatment options.

Diseases We Cover

  • HIV/AIDS
  • Hypertension
  • Diabetes
  • Cancer

HIV/AIDS

Symptoms: Flu-like symptoms, rapid weight loss, swollen lymph nodes.

Treatment: Antiretroviral therapy (ART) to manage the virus.

Hypertension

Symptoms: Severe headaches, dizziness, chest pain.

Treatment: Medication, low-sodium diet, regular exercise.

Diabetes

Symptoms: Increased thirst, frequent urination, fatigue.

Treatment: Insulin therapy, healthy eating, exercise.

Cancer

Symptoms: Unexplained weight loss, lumps, persistent pain.

Treatment: Chemotherapy, radiation therapy, surgery.
